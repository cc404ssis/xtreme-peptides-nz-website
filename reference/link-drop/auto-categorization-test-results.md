- **Progression is cumulative:** Each level builds on the previous
- **Documentation is critical:** SKILL.md files are the bridge from Level 1 to Level 2
- **Error handling differentiates levels:** Higher levels handle failures without human intervention
- **Parallelization at Level 5:** True scale comes from coordinated multi-agent systems
